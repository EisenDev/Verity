# VERITY: Precision Hospitality Intelligence System
## System Documentation v4.0 (Sovereign Engine Refactor)

### 1. MISSION OVERVIEW
VERITY is a high-density, AI-driven hospitality audit and intelligence platform designed for B2B Micro SaaS operations. Built on a foundation of "Luxury Enterprise Aesthetics," it provides real-time forensic analysis of hotel operations across multiple departments, including Housekeeping, Maintenance, and Food & Beverage (F&B).

### 2. CORE ARCHITECTURE (THE SOVEREIGN ENGINE)
The system operates on a "Sovereign Engine" architecture, which prioritizes:
- **Low-Latency Forensic Search**: Real-time filtering across 5,000+ records.
- **Glassmorphism UI**: High-end visual fidelity tailored for executive leadership.
- **Intelligence Liaison**: A floating AI assistant (AI Liaison) that integrates with the Gemini Pro Matrix for live operational guidance.
- **Role-Based Governance**: Strict isolation between 'ADMIN' (Global Access) and 'DEPT_USER' (Operational Access) protocols.

### 3. MODULES AND INTERFACES

#### 3.1 COMMAND CENTER (OVERVIEW)
The Command Center serves as the primary operational hub. It displays:
- **KPI Dashboards**: Dynamic cards showing Global Quality Score, Pending Remediation totals, and Total Financial Risk (Admin Only).
- **The Forensic Table**: A high-density data grid containing audit IDs, timestamps, department labels, inspector names, and analytical ratings.
- **Row-Level Extraction**: Clicking any audit row triggers the "Forensic Slide-Over Drawer," revealing itemized remediation costs and AI-calculated evidence.

#### 3.2 THE TRIAGE ROOM (OPERATIONS)
The Triage Room is a focused view designed for immediate intervention. It filters the global database to show ONLY "Critical" issues (1-star rating with negative sentiment) and "Rating Discrepancies" (5-star rating with negative sentiment). This allows management to prioritize remediation where it is most needed.

#### 3.3 PERFORMANCE INTELLIGENCE (ANALYTICS)
Analytics provides a macro view of the organization:
- **Global Sentiment Base**: A pie chart visualizing the distribution of positive, neutral, and negative sentiment.
- **Velocity Timeline**: A line graph tracking audit volume and negative flag trends over the last 10 days.
- **Department Matrix**: Comparative bars showing the average performance ratings across Housekeeping, Maintenance, and F&B.

### 4. THE AI LIAISON PROTOCOLS
The AI Liaison is the system's "Brain." It is governed by a strict context-locked matrix:
- **Context Binding**: The AI can ONLY answer questions related to the VERITY system, audit data, and hospitality remediation protocols defined in this documentation.
- **Remediation Costing**: When an inspector logs a finding (e.g., "Broken AC unit"), the AI automatically generates an itemized JSON breakdown of costs (parts, labor, urgency fees).
- **Real-Time Price Guard**: Prices are verified via the LUME Procurement API (Simulated) to ensure fiscal accuracy in remediation totals.

### 5. DATA GOVERNANCE AND SECURITY
- **The Gatekeeper**: Authentication is handled via secure tokens. Roles are derived from the server-side session and cannot be spoofed in the UI.
- **Redaction Logic**: Department users (DEPT_USER) are automatically restricted from viewing financial data and global risk metrics.
- **Persistence**: All itemizations and remediation costs are stored in the PostgreSQL database. Regenerating a report updates the persistent record.

### 6. OPERATIONAL WORKFLOW
1. **Inspection**: An inspector visits a department and logs findings and ratings.
2. **Analysis**: The Sovereign Engine processes the data, performs sentiment analysis, and tags discrepancies.
3. **Drafting**: AI Liaison generates the initial itemized remediation cost based on the findings text.
4. **Goverance**: A manager reviews the findings in the Slide-Over Drawer, manually adjusts costs if necessary, and marks the item as "Resolved."
5. **Intelligence**: The data feeds back into the Analytics module for quarterly performance reviews.

### 7. EXPORTING INTELLIGENCE
The system supports executive-level reporting in multiple formats:
- **PDF Executive Report**: A styled document for board-level presentations.
- **Excel Raw Data**: A spreadsheet containing granular line items for the finance and procurement teams.
- **Date-Range Filtering**: Reports can be generated for specific days, weeks, or months to track remediation efficiency over time.
